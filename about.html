Welcome to Monastic International Academy Estd. 2076

Monastic International Academy Estd. 2076 was founded with a dream: to provide quality education in a nurturing environment. Over the years, we have grown into a family of dedicated teachers, enthusiastic students, and supportive parents.

We believe that education goes beyond textbooks – it’s about building character, creativity, and confidence. Our campus is a safe, inclusive space where every child can discover their potential.

Join us as we continue to shape future leaders with values and vision.
